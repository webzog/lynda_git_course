How to apply for credit

  1. You access the American General site.
  2. You choose a store location and complete the credit application.
  3. You will receive the credit decision at your e-mail address.
  4. You will print out the approval and take it to the store location you have chosen.
  5. You will need to show your ID and approval print out form.
